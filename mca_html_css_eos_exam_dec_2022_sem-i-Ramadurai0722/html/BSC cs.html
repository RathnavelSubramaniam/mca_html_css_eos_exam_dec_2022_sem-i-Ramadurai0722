BSC CS ADMMISSION

The MSc Computer Science Admission process usually starts in February or March every year. The registration process for BHU will start in the first week of February, 2022 and aspirants can fill registration forms before March, 2022. Students should hold a bachelor's degree in science stream in any subject from a recognized university. Though graduates with a BCA or a B.Sc in Computer Science have an advantage. A bachelor's degree with a minimum mark of 50 percent is required.

COURSE FEEES

The average fee to pursue BSc in Computer Science is INR 40,000-80,000 per annum.Students who have passed Class 12th in PCB or PCM stream can take admission in B.Sc courses. There are various government and private colleges that offer B.Sc courses in different specializations

SYLLABUS

BSc Computer Science syllabus covers all the important aspects to train the students to make a career in the computer science and software industry. The course curriculum of this course is divided into 6 semesters. BSc Computer Science Subjects include Operating Systems concepts, Introduction to Computer Network, Introduction to Data Structures, Numerical Analysis, System Software, System Programming, Python programming, Introduction to Web Technology etc.
